# Nemo 公众号长文标题

Use this reference when generating, selecting, or checking titles for Nemo-style WeChat/公众号 longform, especially technical personal-IP essays, project retrospectives, AI tool practice articles, and business-judgment articles grounded in real work.

This reference adapts the local DBS content database under `02_Sources/_external/dbskill/知识库/Skill知识包/` and the 2026-06-27 public-account growth notes under `02_Sources/_clippings/` for Nemo's public-account longform. DBS title thinking is useful here, but do not copy Xiaohongshu/short-video bait directly.

## Core Rule

The title is not a conversion prompt by default, but it must give a real click reason.

For Nemo's WeChat longform:

```text
入口让给读者，判断留给自己。
标题负责让读者知道为什么现在值得点开；
正文必须用 Nemo 的真实实践、踩坑、效果、判断变化或边界兑现这个点击。
```

Use this priority order:

1. **Reader entrance**: what search need, hotspot, mistake, cost, failure, curiosity, or practical question makes the right reader open this now?
2. **Article truth**: can the body prove or repay that entrance?
3. **Nemo fit**: does it sound like a technical friend with field judgment, not Xiaohongshu bait, sales copy, or generic consulting?

If a title has Nemo fit but no reader entrance, mark it as flat. If a title has click stimulus but the article cannot repay it, mark it as title bait.

## 10-Point Title Scale

Score every candidate before recommending it.

| Dimension | 0 | 1 | 2 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Reader entrance | Only what Nemo wants to express | Topic exists, but the reason to click now is fuzzy | Clear hotspot, search need, repeated mistake, cost, pitfall, failure consequence, or practical curiosity |
| Concrete object | Abstract topic words only | Has a domain object such as AI product, Skill, Codex, OpenClaw | Has object plus action or scene, such as pricing, writing a price page, wiring WeChat, running a workflow |
| Judgment tension | Only names the topic | Has a mild contrast | Names a wrong default action, misconception, counterintuitive judgment, or deeper question |
| Body repayment | Body cannot prove the promise | Body partially supports the promise | Body has real use, pitfall, effect, judgment change, case, or boundary |
| Nemo fit | Sounds like sales copy, generic consulting, or bait | Accurate but generic | Restrained, concrete, field-tested, and recognizably Nemo |

Decision:

- `8-10`: recommend.
- `6-7`: usable direction, but must be strengthened.
- `0-5`: do not use.

## Nemo Title Shapes

Use these shapes as working families. Do not force all titles into one formula.

### 1. Wrong Action + Corrective Judgment

Use when the article corrects a common first move.

- `别再把 {对象} 当 {错误理解} 了`
- `{领域}，别先问 {表层问题}，先问 {真正问题}`
- `别急着 {错误动作}，先想清楚 {真正问题}`

Examples:

- `别再把 Skill 当提示词包了`
- `AI 产品定价，别先问价格，先问你到底卖什么`
- `别再让 AI 对话白白蒸发`

### 2. You Think A, Actually B

Use when the draft has a sharp mechanism shift.

- `你以为卡在 {A}，其实卡在 {B}`
- `{对象} 最容易错的，不是 {A}，是 {B}`
- `{旧理解} 不等于 {真实机制}`

Examples:

- `你以为卡在模型，其实卡在协作`
- `AI 产品最容易定错的，不是价格，是责任边界`
- `4 个 Bot 不等于团队`

### 3. I Did X, Found Y

Use when the source has first-hand work, debugging, or implementation.

- `我用 {工具} 做了 {事}`
- `我把 {系统/工具} 接到 {场景} 上`
- `我实测/跑通/踩完 {对象}，发现 {判断}`

Examples:

- `我用 AI 给自己造了个公众号发布助手`
- `我把 Codex 接到手机上`
- `装完 OpenClaw 几天烧掉几百块，我是怎么把账单救回来的`

### 4. Pain Scene + Practical Result

Use when the article solves a visible operational pain.

- `{痛点场景}？{具体动作/工具} 解决它`
- `{对象} 太 {成本/风险}？先改 {具体动作}`
- `{数量/现象}，真正能 {结果} 的有几个？`

Examples:

- `收藏夹里 1000 篇文章，真正能找回来的有几篇？`
- `OpenClaw 太烧钱？4 个动作把成本砍掉 50%~95%`
- `网站 Google 搜不到？3 步让搜索引擎收录你的内容`

### 5. Concrete Object + Key Mechanism

Use when the title should name the tool and the mechanism together.

- `{工具} 给 {对象} 补了一层 {机制}`
- `{对象} 总 {问题}？这套 {机制} 让它 {结果}`
- `{工具 A} + {工具 B}：把 {材料} 变成 {资产/流程}`

Examples:

- `OMX 给 Codex 补了一层工作流`
- `Agent 总忘事？这套三层记忆系统，让 5 个 Agent 连跑 30 天不掉线`
- `OpenClaw + Obsidian：把聊天记录变成可复用数字资产的完整工作流`

## Upgrade Method

When a title is accurate but flat, do not add hype. Sharpen the real tension.

```text
correct-but-flat title
-> expose the mistaken default action
-> name the deeper question, cost, or consequence
-> keep the tone calm
```

Example:

- Flat: `AI 产品定价，别先从价格页开始`
- Stronger: `AI 产品定价，别先问价格，先问你到底卖什么`
- More mechanism-heavy: `AI 产品最容易定错的，不是价格，是责任边界`

## Diagnosis Rules

Use these labels when checking titles:

- `自嗨`: the title only shows what Nemo wants to say, with no reader entrance.
- `空泛`: the title uses abstract nouns such as 底层逻辑, 商业本质, 认知升级, 破局, 闭环, 赋能, but no concrete object or action.
- `同质化资讯`: the title rides a hotspot but only promises news, not Nemo's use, pitfall, effect, judgment, or boundary.
- `标题党`: the title creates curiosity or fear that the article cannot repay.
- `小红书味过重`: the title uses over-broad bait such as 普通人必看, 建议收藏, 保姆级, 90% 的人都错了, 看完就懂, unless the article is explicitly a tutorial and the source supports that tone.
- `成交味过重`: the title makes the reader feel they are entering a funnel when the target is personal-IP thought display.

## Forbidden Defaults

Avoid these unless the article explicitly criticizes or substantiates them:

- `底层逻辑`
- `商业本质`
- `认知升级`
- `破局`
- `闭环`
- `赋能`
- `一文讲透`
- `普通人必看`
- `建议收藏`
- `90% 的人都错了`
- `保姆级`
- `看完就懂`

## Output Pattern

When the user asks for title work, return:

1. `推荐标题`: one primary title with a score, such as `8/10`.
2. `为什么`: 1-3 sentences explaining the reader entrance, judgment tension, and body repayment.
3. `备选`: 3-6 alternatives grouped by title family when useful.
4. `不要用`: 1-3 weak directions, labeled with `自嗨`, `空泛`, `同质化资讯`, `标题党`, `小红书味过重`, or `成交味过重`.

When drafting a full article, silently use this reference to choose a title. Only show title alternatives if the user asks.
